Cognitionteam

Designing a super-premium tech brand.

Challenge

Cognitionteam is a relatively young start-up in the ecosystem, given the company's domain, the website is the initial point of contact for clients. A significant difficulty was outlining the company's goal as well as all of the services that Cognitionteam needed to provide to its customers. Cognitionteam provides a broad range of services, which needed to be well-structured on the website so that visitors had a clear grasp of Cognitionteam's work and found the platform simple to use.

Solution

We developed a new logo and an entirely new identity system: colors, fonts, layouts and graphics. We also produced an image library of over 300 photographs, creating a distinctive look of blurred motion in urban environments to represent Crisp’s always-on, constantly moving nature.

Background

Cognitionteam is a technological consulting firm that specialises in software development. I joined this business back in October 2020. Cognitiontionteam did not have a website or portal at that time period. I worked on this project as the only UX/UI designer and designed the whole website from the ground up.In all, four months were devoted to design and development, after which the prototypes were put into development in February 2021.

Defining the Strategy

We created an online style guide to inform Crisp’s internal team in Leeds, along with its risk analysts and representatives around the globe. We update this guide regularly, evolving as new needs arise to ensure brand consistency in voice and look.

Findings from Interviews & Discussions

With the knowledge of the Design Thinking & Software industry practices, I conducted interviews/discussions with the existing/current clients of Cognitionteam, with a pool of personnel, mostly directors, project-leads, who out-source work to companies like Cognitionteam, and also several other employees working in a similar company.

 

Building empathy

Using the quantitative and qualitative data from interviews and discusions results, I defined the three target group profiles David (CTO, 48), Marcos (CEO, 31) and Rachel (Project manager, 35) to better empathize with my main user groups and prioritize goals according to their needs.

Information Architecture

I created the sitemap for Cognitionteam based on the insights obtained from the initial content audits, competition analyses, and Card Sorts with potential users, and then tested it using tree tests with potential users.
